Rewriting Luxury

phantom

A commanding presence, Phantom Family encapsulates contemporary luxury. Its signature
design is complemented by modern features, with the potential to personalise it to the smallest
detail. Born of the desire to build The Best Car in the World, Phantom is the result of
complete creative and engineering freedom.

When it came to designing the current Phantom Family, it’s fitting that the influence came from
Sir Henry Royce himself: “Strive for perfection in everything you do. Take the best that exists
and make it better. When it doesn’t exist, design it.” From that challenge, Phantom was born.
And the result is the definition of automotive design and technological perfection.
